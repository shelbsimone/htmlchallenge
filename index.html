About Shelby

Shelby Williams is about to be the voice of her generation. She is, to be quite frank, one of the best of all of the information systems majors in the world, if not galaxy. Born and raised in the Buckeye state of Columbus, Ohio, she has a demonstrated history of creativity which she strives to bring into the technology industry. She has interned with companies like PromoWest Productions, Accenture Federal Services, and Glass Axis, to name a few.

Her biggest goal is to become a user experience specialist. She wants to conquer and understand the psychology behing the ways in which people use technology and how it makes them feel. She aims to use this knowledge in order to develop useful applications for the tech-savvy at-risk youth around the world.
